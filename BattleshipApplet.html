Battleship

by Brendan Murphy and John Bowllan

First, place all four ships on the right grid by selecting a ship from the 'Ship' drop down menu, selecting a direction from the 'Direction' drop down menu, and clicking on an valid square on the right grid. Then click the 'Set my ships' button and begin playing the CPU. To strike, click a square on the left grid and press the 'Strike!' button. If you are unsatisfied with your choice, press the highlighted square to unhighlight and select another square. First player to sink all of the opponents ships wins! GOOD LUCK!
